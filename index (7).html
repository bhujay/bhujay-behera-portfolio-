About Me

The Digital Dreamer
from Odisha

Born on 24th August 2000 in Paradip, Odisha, I grew up in Singitali Village, Jagatsinghpur — and built a digital career from there. I believe that ambition backed by effort is its own infrastructure.

I've worked with Mervice Infotech Pvt. Ltd., Altruist Technologies Pvt. Ltd., and Home Credit India Finance Pvt. Ltd. — sharpening skills in client communication, financial support, and technology.

Today I blog, build websites, invest in stock markets and crypto, and inspire young people across Odisha to pursue digital careers without leaving home.
